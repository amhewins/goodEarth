When Good Earth Grains & Creamery opened in July 2009, the small town of Coopersburg came alive to welcome this unique country store. Located at 23 South Main Street in the historic Fluck’s Hardware Store, Good Earth continues a century long tradition of being a meeting spot for the community. Whether folks come in for the famous Nelson’s (“nix besser”) Ice Cream, the wide selection of prepackaged bulk foods (flours, rice, pasta, dried fruits, beans, spice, trail mixes, candies), for the coffee ( Bucks County’s own organic/fair trade Homestead Roasters), or the incredible Emmaus Bakery baked goods, customers are sure to find neighbors and friends inside or enjoying the sunshine on outdoor benches.

Friendly customer service is the hallmark of Good Earth: Can’t find what you’re looking for? We’ll try to get it. Need a recipe for that quinoa? We’ve got it! Want something packaged in a different size bag? Not a problem. You can even call ahead and just pop in and pick it up!

The mission at Good Earth is that a family of four can enjoy ice cream here for under $10 and …..”Sprinkles are always free” !!

Come on in ….we’re looking forward to meeting you!
